Come Bowl With Us!

Here at Helen Newman Bowling we like to have fun. If you like bowling then you're right up our alley. Whether you're here to play a friendly game with your friends or to compete against other serious bowlers, we have just the thing for you.

There are many things to do here including a food and beverage lounge, arcade games, and a CD jukebox. Bowling shoes are provided as well as bowling balls. While you're here get to know the staff because they always have time to spare. Have fun!
