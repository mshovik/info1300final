About Us

Meet John LaRock

John LaRock may not be a football player, but he is a super-bowler. Seriously, he is a professional bowler. He has bowled a perfect game (12 strikes in a row) and he's even tried out for team USA. He uses his experience to teach students at Cornell University. He's been teaching at Cornell since 2012.

Lanes

There are 16 lanes with a maximum of 5 people per lane. There is no automated score keeping, but you can either use paper and pencil score keeping or visit a score keeping app to do it semi-automatically.Bumpers are available only for children.
